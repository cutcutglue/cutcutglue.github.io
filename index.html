The Process

I use a variety of design applications, and different media to create my customized orders. When making cards, I will primarily use cardstock, but can work with various other materials of your choosing. Cutting boards and charcuterie trays are made by burning your design into different types of wood. All boards are coated with a food safe oil, and are safe for both eating and preparing food. If you have a custom design in mind, please feel free to contact me below; I can work with paper, wood or vinyl.
